Benefits of Exercise

Exercising during pregnancy offers many benefits, including:

  • Fewer pregnancy-related complications such as:
    • Gestational diabetes
    • Preeclampsia
    • Excessive weight gain
  • More strength and endurance for the birthing process
  • Faster births
  • Faster postpartum recovery
  • Fewer complications during birth
  • Lower rates of cesarean deliveries
  • Feeling better, both physically and mentally
  • Reduced symptoms of depression and anxiety
