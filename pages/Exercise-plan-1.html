Weeks 1-2 of Exercise Plan

    Monday
  • Water Aerobics- 30 min
    • Warm up: 10 minutes
    • High knee “walk” across shallow end of pool for 2 minutes
    • Arm circles for 1 minute
    • 2 minutes kicking across pool using kickboard for support
    • Gently swim laps for 5 minutes (stroke of choice)
    Workout - 30 min
  • Circuit 1
    • Fast wall-kicks- 1.5 minutes min
    • Pool push-ups- 1 min
    • Water jogging- 1.5 min
    • Water lunges- 1.5 min
    • Opposite leg-arm kicks- 1.5 min
    • Forward/side balance leg lifts 1.5 min
    • Swim to deep end, tread water for 30 sec, swim back to shallow end. (1.5 min total)
    • Rest 30 seconds
  • Repeat circuit
    • Fast wall-kicks- 1.5 minutes min
    • Pool push-ups- 1 min
    • Water jogging- 1.5 min
    • Water lunges- 1.5 min
    • Opposite leg-arm kicks- 1.5 min
    • Forward/side balance leg lifts 1.5 min
    • Swim to deep end, tread water for 30 sec, swim back to shallow end. (1.5 min total)
    • Rest 30 seconds
  • Cool Down - 10 min
    • 10 minutes warmup laps using kick board for support
    • 20 minutes laps without kick board (stroke of choice)
    • 10 minute cool down with kickboard
    Wednesday: Low-impact Aerobics/Weight Circuit
    Equipment needed: Sturdy bench or step, light-medium dumbbells, resistance band
  • Warm Up - 10 min
    • Warm up- 10 min
    • Walk on treadmill, or on flat sidewalk, for 6 min. Treadmill speed suggestion: about 3mph, incline 0.
    • Arm circles- 1 min
    • March in place 1 min
    • Opposite leg/arm toe touches 15 times on each leg (30 touches total).
    • Standing side lunges- 1 min
  • Workout
    • Circuit A: (5 min)
    • Bench Marching 1 min
    • Squats- 1 min or up to 15 repetitions
    • Modified push-ups (on mat)- 1 min or up to 15 repetitions
    • Opposite arm/leg extensions- 1 min or up to 15 repetitions
    • Bench step-ups to balance -1 min or up to 15 repetitions
  • Rest 1 minute (drink water)
    • Circuit B: (5 minutes)
    • Standing row with resistance band- 1 min or 12-15 repetitions
    • Resistance band lateral stepping- 1 min or 12-15 repetitions
    • Single leg reverse lunges- 1 min or 12-15 repetitions
    • Resistance band bicep curls- 1 min or 12-15 repetitions
    • Tricep extensions with resistance band- 1 min or 12-15 repetitions
  • Rest one minute (drink water)
    • Circuit C (5 min)
    • Bicep curls - 1 min or 12-15 repetitions
    • Tricep extensions- 1 min or 12-15 repetitions
    • Squats with weights- 1 min or 12-15 repetitions
    • Standing Shoulder press - 1 min or 12-15 repetitions
    • Resistance band Lat pull down- 1 min or 12-15 repetitions
  • Rest 1 min (drink water)
    • Repeat Circuit A
    • Rest 1 min
    • Repeat Circuit B
    • Rest 1 min
    • Repeat Circuit C
  • Cool Down-10 min
    • Walk on flat surface or treadmill for 5 minutes (about 3 mph)
    • Standing side lunges 30 sec
    • Arm circles 30 sec
    • Standing hamstring stretch (15 seconds per side)
    • Standing calf stretch (15 seconds per side)
    • Standing quad stretch (15 seconds per side)
    • Standing shoulder stretch (15 seconds per side)
    • Standing triceps stretch (15 seconds per side)
    Friday:
    • Walking/Jog intervals
    • Warm up: Walk for 10 minutes at a comfortable pace
    • 30 min intervals: Alternate between walking at a moderate pace for 2 minutes and light jogging for 2 minutes (should feel like 12-14 on a scale of 6-20)
    • Cool down: Walk for 10 minutes at a comfortable pace

  • Rest for 30 seconds

  • Rest for 30 seconds
    • Wall-kicks. 1 min facing wall and 1 min away- 2 min total
    • Pool push-ups- 1 min or 12-15 repetitions
  • Rest for 30 seconds
    • Water jogging- 1 min
    • Water lunges- 1.5 min or 12-15 repetitions repetitions per leg
    • Forward/side balance leg lifts- 1.5 minutes or 12-15 repetitions per leg
  • Rest for 30 seconds
