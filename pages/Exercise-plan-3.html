Warm up: 10 minutes High knee “walk” across shallow end of pool- 2 min Arm circles for 1 min Kicking across pool using kickboard for support- 7 min

Workout- 25 Wall-kicks. 1 min facing wall and 1 min away- 2 min total Pool push-ups- 1 or 12 reps Rest for 30 seconds

Wednesday Low impact aerobic/resistance training circuit

Warm up- 10 min Walk on treadmill, or on flat sidewalk, for 8 min. Treadmill speed suggestion: about 3mph, incline 0. Arm circles- 1 min March in place- 1 min Opposite leg/arm toe touches. Alternating sides 1 min Standing side lunges- 1 min Repeat, alternating sides- 1 min.

Workout- 25 min Bench Marching 1.5 min Modified standard push-ups with bench- 1.5 minute or 12 push-ups Modified Tricep push-ups with bench- 1.5 min or 12 push-ups Rest 30 seconds

Opposite arm/leg extensions- 2 min Step-back lunges - 2 min or 12 each leg Rest 30 seconds (drink water)

Repeat Bench Marching 1.5 min Modified standard push-ups with bench- 1.5 minute or 12 push-ups Modified Tricep push-ups with bench- 1.5 min or 12 push-ups Rest 30 seconds

Opposite arm/leg extensions- 2 min Step-back lunges - 2 min or 12 each leg Rest 30 seconds

Bench Marching 1.5 min Opposite arm/leg extensions 1.5 min

Cool down- 14.5 minutes Walk on flat surface or treadmill for 10 minutes Standing side lunges- 1 min Arm circles- 1 min Standing hamstring stretch (15 seconds per side) Standing calf stretch (15 seconds per side) Standing quad stretch (15 seconds per side) Standing shoulder stretch (15 seconds per side) Standing triceps stretch (15 seconds per side)
